## Hair Transplant Cost Breakdown in Turkey

### Procedure Types and Pricing

**FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction)**
- **Price Range:** $1,800 - $3,500 USD
- **Grafts Included:** 2,000 - 4,000 grafts
- **Ideal For:** First-time patients, moderate hair loss
- **Recovery Time:** 7-10 days
- **Success Rate:** 90-95%

**DHI (Direct Hair Implantation)**
- **Price Range:** $2,500 - $4,500 USD
- **Grafts Included:** 1,500 - 3,000 grafts
- **Ideal For:** High-density requirements, natural appearance
- **Recovery Time:** 5-7 days
- **Success Rate:** 92-97%

**Robotic FUE**
- **Price Range:** $3,000 - $5,000 USD
- **Grafts Included:** 2,500 - 4,500 grafts
- **Ideal For:** Precision requirements, complex cases
- **Recovery Time:** 6-8 days
- **Success Rate:** 95-98%

- **Price Range:** $2,200 - $3,800 USD
- **Grafts Included:** 2,000 - 4,000 grafts
- **Ideal For:** Minimal trauma, faster healing
- **Recovery Time:** 6-8 days
- **Success Rate:** 93-96%

## Factors Affecting Hair Transplant Costs

### Patient-Specific Factors

**Extent of Hair Loss**
- **Norwood Scale 2-3:** $1,800 - $2,500 (lower range)
- **Norwood Scale 4-5:** $2,500 - $3,500 (mid range)
- **Norwood Scale 6-7:** $3,500 - $5,000+ (higher range)

**Hair Characteristics**
- **Fine hair:** Requires more grafts, higher cost
- **Coarse hair:** Fewer grafts needed, lower cost
- **Curly hair:** More complex implantation, higher cost
- **Previous procedures:** Revision surgeries cost more

**Donor Area Quality**
- **Excellent donor supply:** Lower per-graft cost
- **Limited donor supply:** Higher per-graft cost
- **Scar tissue:** More complex extraction, higher cost
- **Previous surgeries:** Technical difficulty increases cost

### Clinic-Specific Factors

**Location and Reputation**
- **Istanbul clinics:** 10-20% premium pricing
- **Antalya clinics:** Competitive pricing with tourism
- **Izmir clinics:** Mid-range pricing
- **Newer clinics:** Lower initial pricing

**Surgeon Experience**
- **5-10 years experience:** Standard pricing
- **10-15 years experience:** 15-25% premium
- **15+ years experience:** 25-40% premium
- **International training:** Additional premium

**Technology and Facilities**
- **Robotic assistance:** 20-30% premium
- **JCI accreditation:** 10-15% premium
- **Advanced imaging:** 5-10% premium
- **Private hospital:** 10-20% premium
